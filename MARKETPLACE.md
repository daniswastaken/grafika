# Grafika.
**Grafika** is a simple, beautiful shader for Minecraft. It makes everything look better, without making your device slower.

## 🎨 Why Anime-Style?
![By the Fireplace (Loading Image)](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/daniswastaken/hdn@main/grafika/by-the-fireplace.png)
We call it "Anime-Style" because of the **Outlines**. Every player and mob gets a sharp, drawn-style border. It looks just like the clean, bold lines in your favorite anime. It makes characters pop and gives the world a unique, hand-drawn feel.

## ✨ Features

- **The Singularity.** A massive, cinematic black hole in the End. It's majestic. It's scary.
- **Glowing Ores.** High-visibility ores that glows in the dark. Find them fast.
- **Better Lava.** Thick, moving, and realistic. Just like it should be.
- **Fast.** Works on your phone. Works on your PC. Smooth as butter.

## 📁 Subpacks To Choose.
- **Shader Only**: Just the beautiful shaders. No outlines.
- **All Outline**: Outlines for everyone. The full anime experience.
- **Player Outline Only**: Outlines for players only. High focus.

## 📦 Simple to Install.
- **Works on**: Windows, Android, iOS.
- **Needs**: BetterRenderDragon or Minecraft Patched.
- **Install**: One click. That's it.
