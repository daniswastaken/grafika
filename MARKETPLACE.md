# Grafika v2.1

**Grafika** is an anime-styled, simple and lightweight but still beautiful shader for Minecraft.

## Anime-Style

It's "Anime-Style" because of the **Outlines** given to every player that looks like in anime show.

## Features

*   **The Singularity.** Cinematic black hole in the End dimension.
*   **Glowing Ores.** All ores now glow in the dark.
*   **Performance First.** Lightweight and fast on low-end device.

## Subpacks To Choose.

*   **Shader Only**: Just the shader. No outlines.
*   **Player Outline**: Anime-style outlines for players and shader.

## Simple to Install.

*   **Supported On**: Windows, Android, iOS.
*   **Requirements**: **BetterRenderDragon**, **Minecraft Patched**, **Matject**, or **MB Loader**.

# THE PLAYER OUTLINE ONLY WORKS WITH WINDOWS

If you're on an Android or iOS, the OUTLINE will glitch but THE SHADER STILL 100% WORKS. I'm currently fixing it.
